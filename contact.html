Frequently asked questions
Is BulkBro free?
BulkBro is free to download. Some optional features may require a purchase or subscription in the future. Core tracking remains simple and easy to start.
How does BulkBro adjust my calories?
Each week, BulkBro asks you to log your current weight. Based on your logged progress and selected goal pace, BulkBro may update your daily calorie target.
Is my data private?
Yes. We never sell your data. Health data you enter stays in your account and is used only to power your tracking experience. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I delete my account?
Email support@bulkbroapp.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We'll confirm and process it within 30 days.
I found a bug — how do I report it?
Email us at support@bulkbroapp.com with a description of what happened and your device model. We appreciate the reports and fix bugs quickly.
