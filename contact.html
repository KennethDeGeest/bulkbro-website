Account & Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your BulkBro data directly from within the app.

Open BulkBro → Settings → Delete data and follow the confirmation steps.

If you no longer have access to the app, email us at support@bulkbroapp.com and we'll assist you with your deletion request.
