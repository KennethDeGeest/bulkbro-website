Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information BulkBro collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

BulkBro ("we", "our", or "us") provides a calorie and weight tracking app designed to help users track daily calorie intake, body weight progress, streaks, and goal-related activity.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how long we keep it, and how you can request deletion of your data.

2. Data We Collect

When you use BulkBro, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: such as your email address, display name, login method, and profile preferences you provide during setup.
  • Health and fitness information: such as body weight entries, calorie logs, calorie targets, saved meals, streak progress, goal settings, activity level, and other information you choose to enter into the app.
  • Device and diagnostic information: such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash reports, and performance diagnostics.
  • Usage information: such as feature interactions, session activity, screen views, and general analytics used to improve app stability and usability.

BulkBro does not collect government identification numbers, biometric identifiers, or sensitive financial information.

3. How We Use Data

We use collected information to provide and improve BulkBro, including:

  • Calculating and displaying calorie targets.
  • Saving calorie logs, saved meals, weight entries, streaks, and progress history.
  • Providing weekly check-ins and progress tracking.
  • Improving app performance, usability, and reliability.
  • Responding to support, feedback, and account deletion requests.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Analytics and Third-Party Services

BulkBro may use third-party analytics, crash reporting, hosting, database, authentication, and infrastructure providers to operate the app and improve the user experience.

These services may process limited technical information such as device type, app version, crash diagnostics, and anonymous or aggregated usage statistics.

Third-party providers are only permitted to process data as needed to provide services to BulkBro.

5. App Usage and Health Data

Calorie logs, weight entries, goal settings, and streak history are used to provide progress tracking and calorie target features inside BulkBro.

BulkBro is not a medical app and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Information shown in the app is for general tracking and informational purposes only.

6. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your data may be stored using trusted service providers that help us operate the app.

7. Data Retention

We retain account and tracking data for as long as your account remains active or as long as needed to provide BulkBro’s features.

If you request account deletion, associated personal data will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable timeframe, unless we are required to retain certain information for legal, security, or operational reasons.

8. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your BulkBro account and associated data at any time.

Deletion requests can be submitted through the app if available, or by emailing support@bulkbroapp.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

BulkBro is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Users

If you use BulkBro outside your country of residence, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate.

By using BulkBro, you understand that your information may be processed according to this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we may notify users through the app, by email, or by updating the date on this page.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, your data, or account deletion, contact us at support@bulkbroapp.com.
