Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

BulkBro is a nutrition and weight tracking app designed to help users understand whether their bulk is working. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can contact us.

2. Data We Collect

When you use BulkBro, we may collect information you provide in the app, including account details, onboarding answers, body weight entries, calorie logs, calorie targets, saved meals, streak progress, goal settings, and activity preferences.

We may also collect device, analytics, crash, and usage information such as app version, device type, operating system version, screen views, feature interactions, and diagnostic data.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide calorie targets, weight tracking, food logging, progress feedback, and app functionality.
  • To generate bulk-related feedback based on the information you choose to enter.
  • To improve stability, usability, product quality, and support.
  • To understand how users interact with the app in aggregate.

4. Health and Fitness Information

BulkBro may process health and fitness-related information you enter, such as weight, calorie intake, and goal preferences. BulkBro is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

5. Third-Party Services

BulkBro may use trusted third-party services for authentication, analytics, crash reporting, subscriptions, hosting, and app functionality. These services may process limited data according to their own privacy policies and only as needed to provide or improve the app.

6. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain account functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve the product. You may request deletion by contacting us.

7. Your Choices

You can choose what information to enter into BulkBro. You may also request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting support@bulkbroapp.com.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information. No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children

BulkBro is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. The latest version will be posted on this page.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact support@bulkbroapp.com.
